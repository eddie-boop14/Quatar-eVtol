Doha Airport Vertiport (planned)

A vertiport at Hamad International Airport (HIA), Doha, has been referenced only as a future development category in EHang's 17 November 2025 corporate release following Qatar's first urban pilotless eVTOL trial. As of 1 May 2026, no formal announcement has been issued by HIA, Qatar Airways, the Ministry of Transport (MoT) or the Qatar Civil Aviation Authority (QCAA).

Présentation

The Doha Airport Vertiport is a conceptual electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) facility referenced in connection with Hamad International Airport (HIA, IATA: DOH; ICAO: OTHH), Doha, Qatar. The reference originates from a single forward-looking statement in EHang's corporate release of 17 November 2025, which lists 'shuttle services between airport and urban centers' as a focus area for future development in Qatar following the November 2025 trial campaign.

As of 1 May 2026, no formal announcement of an HIA vertiport has been issued by HIA, Qatar Airways, the Ministry of Transport, or the Qatar Civil Aviation Authority. HIA's published 2025 operational review describes 2026 priorities as system optimisation and runway maintenance, with no mention of vertiport infrastructure. This entity is recorded for completeness; its status and details remain unconfirmed.

Questions fréquentes

Is the Doha Airport Vertiport actually planned?
Doha Airport Vertiport is referenced only as a conceptual future development category in EHang's 17 November 2025 corporate release. As of 1 May 2026, no formal announcement has been issued by Hamad International Airport, Qatar Airways, the Ministry of Transport, or the QCAA. The status is planned only in an aspirational sense.
Who will operate the Doha Airport Vertiport?
No operator has been announced for the Doha Airport Vertiport. EHang has stated an intention to develop a 'strategic alliance' with Qatar's Ministry of Transport for future eVTOL deployment, but no operator selection has been made public.
When will the Doha Airport Vertiport open?
No opening date has been published for the Doha Airport Vertiport. No construction has begun, no builder has been named, and no design has been released by HIA or any Qatar authority.
What aircraft will use the Doha Airport Vertiport?
No aircraft has been formally designated for the Doha Airport Vertiport. The EHang EH216-S was used in Qatar's November 2025 urban trials; whether the same aircraft or a different certified eVTOL will serve future airport operations has not been announced.
Where is the Doha Airport Vertiport located within HIA?
No specific siting within Hamad International Airport has been published for the Doha Airport Vertiport. Coordinates listed (25.2731° N, 51.6080° E) reference the HIA airport itself, not a vertiport-specific location.
Has Qatar Airways commented on the Doha Airport Vertiport?
No. As of 1 May 2026, Qatar Airways has not issued any public statement regarding a Doha Airport Vertiport on its corporate or HIA press channels.
