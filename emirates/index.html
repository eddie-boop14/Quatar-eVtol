— The method

The method manifesto

Most eVTOL coverage online is aggregated press-release news. This site is not that. Every claim on every page traces to a named primary source — an operator press release, a regulator filing, a government statement, a flight test announcement from the company itself.

When EHang says "shuttle services between airport and urban centers" without naming a corridor, we record that — not a fake corridor invented to make a page look complete. When Joby has a Memorandum of Understanding with Dubai but no exclusive contract, we record what the MoU says and what it does not say.

Pages in Arabic, French, German and Chinese are auto-translated from the canonical English. The original sources stay in their original language — every source link goes to the primary document. Where a human translator has hand-authored a translation, the auto-translation banner is removed.

Independent project. Not affiliated with Joby, Archer, EHang, Volocopter, Wisk, RTA, GCAA, or any operator or regulator listed on the site.
