Archer Midnight — UAE Launch Edition Flight Test Campaign

Archer Aviation's Midnight eVTOL completed a full-envelope in-country flight test campaign in the UAE through 14 November 2025, marking the most extensive eVTOL desert flight test program publicly disclosed. Présentation

The Archer Midnight is a four-passenger plus one-pilot eVTOL aircraft built by Archer Aviation (NYSE:ACHR). The UAE Launch Edition program covers in-country flight testing, certification readiness, and pre-commercial operations with Abu Dhabi Aviation (ADA) as the operational partner.

The Midnight conducted its first piloted flight in Abu Dhabi at Al Bateen Executive Airport (AZI) on 2 July 2025. A subsequent multi-month full-envelope desert flight test campaign culminated in Archer's 14 November 2025 announcement of completion. According to Archer's IR release, this was the most extensive in-country eVTOL flight test program publicly disclosed, covering desert temperature, dust, and density-altitude conditions.

Archer's framework agreement with the Abu Dhabi Investment Office (ADIO) was signed at DRIFTx Abu Dhabi on 25 April 2024. Archer's 14 November 2025 IR release confirms that ADIO has now activated this as a definitive agreement, with first payments flowing from Abu Dhabi Aviation under the Launch Edition program.

Statut réglementaire

The Archer Midnight in UAE operations falls under the General Civil Aviation Authority (GCAA), with the Integrated Transport Centre (ITC) providing Abu Dhabi-level oversight. GCAA's air-taxi certification target is Q3 2026. The Cruise Terminal Hybrid Heliport (design approved 23 April 2025) operates under the GCAA's pioneering hybrid-infrastructure framework — the first such framework globally per Archer's release.

Avancement actuel

As of 20 May 2026, Archer's Midnight has completed its full-envelope desert flight test campaign in the UAE. The Launch Edition definitive agreement with ADA is now active with first payments flowing. Commercial operations are gated on GCAA aircraft type certification, targeted for Q3 2026.

Questions fréquentes

What is the Archer Midnight aircraft?
The Archer Midnight is an electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) aircraft built by Archer Aviation (NYSE:ACHR). It is a fixed-wing tilt-rotor design carrying one pilot and four passengers.
When did Archer Midnight first fly in Abu Dhabi?
The first piloted Archer Midnight flight in Abu Dhabi took place at Al Bateen Executive Airport (AZI) on 2 July 2025. A subsequent full-envelope desert flight test campaign concluded on 14 November 2025.
Who operates Archer Midnight in the UAE?
Abu Dhabi Aviation (ADA) is the named eVTOL operator under Archer's Launch Edition definitive agreement, now in payment phase as of November 2025.
When will Archer Midnight start commercial operations in UAE?
Commercial operations are gated on GCAA aircraft type certification, which Director-General Saif Mohammed Al Suwaidi indicated is targeted for Q3 2026.
What was tested in the UAE flight campaign?
The Launch Edition program tested the Midnight under desert conditions including high temperature, dust, and density-altitude — the most extensive in-country eVTOL flight test program publicly disclosed, per Archer's 14 November 2025 release.
Is Archer in Saudi Arabia too?
Archer has signed MoUs with Saudi Arabia's GACA (10 December 2025) and with PIF's The Helicopter Company / Red Sea Global (19 November 2025). These are framework agreements only — no binding aircraft purchase order with a Saudi entity has been disclosed.
