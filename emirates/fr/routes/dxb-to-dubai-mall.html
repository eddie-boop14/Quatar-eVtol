DXB ↔ Dubai Mall

The DXB ↔ Dubai Mall route is a planned corridor in the initial Dubai eVTOL network operated by Joby Aviation under its exclusive agreement with the Roads and Transport Authority. The Dubai Mall vertiport, located at the Zabeel Dubai Mall parking area and developed by Emaar Properties, is one of the three additional vertiports announced 17 November 2025. Specific flight time has not been published by Joby or RTA.

Présentation

The DXB ↔ Dubai Mall route is a planned eVTOL corridor between Dubai International Airport and the Dubai Mall vertiport in Downtown Dubai. It is one of the corridors created by the initial four-vertiport network announced jointly by the RTA, Joby Aviation and Skyports Infrastructure on 17 November 2025: DXB, Dubai Mall, Atlantis the Royal, and American University of Dubai.

The Dubai Mall vertiport is located at the Zabeel Dubai Mall parking area and is being developed in partnership with Emaar Properties. As of May 2026, neither Joby Aviation nor the RTA has published a specific expected flight time for the DXB ↔ Dubai Mall corridor. By contrast, the DXB ↔ Palm Jumeirah corridor has been quoted by Joby (12 minutes) and RTA (~10 minutes). Both endpoint vertiports must be completed before commercial service begins, with launch targeted for 2026.

Comment s’y rendre

En métro

Dubai Metro Red Line connects Dubai International Airport (Airport Terminal 1 / Airport Terminal 3 stations) to Burj Khalifa / Dubai Mall station, which serves the Dubai Mall area.

En voiture

DXB to Dubai Mall is approximately 13 km via Sheikh Rashid Road / Financial Centre Road. Driving time varies materially with traffic.

Depuis l’aéroport

DXB Vertiport is at Dubai International Airport itself — the route originates here.

Statut réglementaire

Operations on the DXB ↔ Dubai Mall route will fall under the General Civil Aviation Authority (GCAA) for airworthiness and the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) Dubai for the operational franchise, under Joby Aviation's six-year exclusive agreement signed February 2024. The Dubai Civil Aviation Authority (DCAA) is also involved in the operational framework. As of May 2026, no specific route certification has been published — certification covers the operator and aircraft type at network level.

Avancement actuel

The DXB vertiport reached its highest structural point and was reported by Skyports Infrastructure to be 60% complete as of November 2025, with Q1 2026 completion scheduled. The Dubai Mall vertiport at the Zabeel parking area was at announcement / early planning stage when reported on 17 November 2025. Commercial service on this corridor depends on both endpoints being delivered, plus operator and aircraft certification.

Questions fréquentes

How long will the DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL flight take?
Specific flight time for the DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL route has not been published by Joby Aviation or the Roads and Transport Authority. The DXB to Palm Jumeirah corridor has been quoted at 10–12 minutes; Dubai Mall is geographically closer to DXB, but no operator-published figure exists for this corridor.
When will the DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL route open?
The DXB to Dubai Mall route is targeted for 2026 in line with the broader Dubai air taxi network commercial launch. A specific opening date has not been published. The DXB vertiport is scheduled for Q1 2026 completion; the Dubai Mall vertiport was at planning stage when announced on 17 November 2025.
Where will the DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL route start and end?
The DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL route will run between the DXB vertiport at Dubai International Airport and the Dubai Mall vertiport at the Zabeel Dubai Mall parking area, developed in partnership with Emaar Properties.
Who operates the DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL route?
The DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL route will be operated by Joby Aviation under the six-year exclusive air taxi agreement with the Roads and Transport Authority of Dubai, signed February 2024.
What aircraft will fly the DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL route?
The DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL route will use the Joby S4, an all-electric piloted aircraft carrying one pilot plus four passengers. The S4 is the only aircraft authorised under Joby's exclusive Dubai operating agreement during the six-year exclusivity period.
How much will the DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL flight cost?
Pricing for the DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL flight has not been published by Joby Aviation or the RTA. No operator or regulator has confirmed a fare.
Is the DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL route operational yet?
No. As of May 2026, the DXB to Dubai Mall eVTOL route is not operational. The DXB vertiport is under construction with Q1 2026 completion scheduled; the Dubai Mall vertiport is at planning stage.
