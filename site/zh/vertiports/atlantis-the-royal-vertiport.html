Atlantis The Royal Vertiport

Atlantis The Royal Vertiport is the Palm Jumeirah node of Dubai's initial four-site eVTOL network, announced on 17 November 2025 by Joby Aviation, the Roads and Transport Authority and Skyports Infrastructure during Dubai Airshow 2025. The vertiport will be built within the Atlantis The Royal hotel under an agreement signed by RTA with the property's owner. As of 1 May 2026 no groundbreaking, design reveal, capacity figure or opening date specific to this site has been published.

简介

The Atlantis The Royal Vertiport is a planned eVTOL facility within the Atlantis The Royal hotel on Palm Jumeirah, Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It serves as the Palm Jumeirah node of the initial four-site Dubai eVTOL network alongside the DXB Vertiport, Dubai Mall Vertiport and American University of Dubai Vertiport.

The site was announced on 17 November 2025 during Dubai Airshow 2025 by Joby Aviation, the Roads and Transport Authority of Dubai (RTA) and Skyports Infrastructure. The RTA signed an agreement with Atlantis The Royal to host the vertiport within the property. Skyports Infrastructure will design, build and operate the vertiport. Joby Aviation will operate the air taxi service under a six-year exclusive agreement with the RTA signed at the World Governments Summit on 11 February 2024.

As of 1 May 2026 no groundbreaking has been publicly reported for this site. Joby's Q4 2025 earnings call on 25 February 2026 described only the DXB and American University of Dubai vertiports as nearing completion. The aircraft will be the Joby S4. Specific opening date, pad configuration, capacity figures, GPS coordinates and pricing have not been published in any primary source.

地铁

Palm Jumeirah is served by the Palm Monorail, not the Dubai Metro. Specific access details from the monorail to the vertiport have not been published by the operator.

驾车

Atlantis The Royal is on Crescent Road, Palm Jumeirah, Dubai. Access via Crescent Road West. Specific kerbside arrangements for the vertiport have not been published.

从机场出发

RTA states the eVTOL flight from Dubai International Airport to Palm Jumeirah will take approximately 10 minutes versus roughly 45 minutes by car. Network-level statement; no schedule has been published.

监管状态

The air taxi service operates under a six-year exclusive agreement between Joby Aviation and the RTA Dubai signed on 11 February 2024 at the World Governments Summit. Vertiport infrastructure falls under the UAE General Civil Aviation Authority (GCAA) regulatory framework (CAR-HVD Part III Vertiport Regulations). Air navigation oversight is provided by Dubai Civil Aviation Authority (DCAA) and Dubai Air Navigation Services (DANS). No site-specific GCAA approval for Atlantis The Royal Vertiport has been publicly reported as of the verification date.
