Dubai Mall Vertiport

Dubai Mall Vertiport was announced on 17 November 2025 by Joby Aviation, the RTA and Skyports Infrastructure as one of three new sites added to the initial Dubai eVTOL network. Emaar Properties is the named property partner. Construction is set to begin in the months following Skyports' 16 April 2026 update, under Joby's six-year exclusive RTA agreement.

About

Dubai Mall Vertiport is a planned electric vertical take-off and landing (eVTOL) facility at the Dubai Mall in Downtown Dubai. It was announced on 17 November 2025 by Joby Aviation, alongside two further sites — Atlantis the Royal and the American University of Dubai — as part of the initial Dubai vertiport network being built by Skyports Infrastructure under contract to the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA). Emaar Properties is the named property partner.

Joby holds an exclusive six-year operational agreement with the RTA covering air taxi services in the Emirate, signed on 11 February 2024 at the World Governments Summit. Joby's 17 November 2025 release describes the Dubai Mall site as 'the world's largest shopping and entertainment destination, attracting nearly 111 million visitors in 2024'. Skyports stated on 16 April 2026 that construction at the Dubai Mall and Palm Jumeirah nodes was set to begin in the coming months, following the technical completion of DXV.

By metro

Dubai Metro Red Line — Burj Khalifa / Dubai Mall station serves the area.

By car

Downtown Dubai, accessible via Sheikh Zayed Road and Financial Centre Road. Vertiport-specific access details not yet published.

From the airport

Approximately 15 km by road from Dubai International Airport (DXB). A direct DXB ↔ Dubai Mall eVTOL connection is part of the announced network.

Regulatory status

Operations will fall under the General Civil Aviation Authority (GCAA) of the UAE for airworthiness and the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) for the operational franchise, working alongside the Dubai Civil Aviation Authority (DCAA) and Dubai Air Navigation Services (DANS). Joby's six-year exclusive agreement with the RTA was signed 11 February 2024 at the World Governments Summit and grants exclusive right to operate air taxis in Dubai for six years from commercial launch.

Current progress

As of 1 May 2026, the Dubai Mall site has been announced and identified with Emaar Properties as property partner. No vertiport-specific construction milestones have been published. Frequently asked questions

When will Dubai Mall Vertiport open?
Dubai Mall Vertiport is planned to be part of Joby Aviation's commercial eVTOL network launching in Dubai in 2026. Skyports stated on 16 April 2026 that construction at the Dubai Mall node was set to begin in the coming months. A specific opening date for the Dubai Mall site has not been published.
Who operates Dubai Mall Vertiport?
Dubai Mall Vertiport will be operated by Joby Aviation under the company's six-year exclusive air taxi agreement with the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) of Dubai, signed 11 February 2024 at the World Governments Summit. The vertiport is being built by Skyports Infrastructure, with Emaar Properties as the property partner.
Who owns the land for Dubai Mall Vertiport?
Emaar Properties is named by Joby Aviation and the RTA as the property partner for Dubai Mall Vertiport. Joby's 17 November 2025 release identifies Emaar alongside Atlantis The Royal and Wasl Asset Management Group as the three property partners for the next three vertiports.
What aircraft will fly from Dubai Mall Vertiport?
Dubai Mall Vertiport will serve the Joby S4, an all-electric vertical take-off and landing aircraft carrying one pilot and four passengers at speeds up to 200 mph. The S4 is the only aircraft authorised under Joby's exclusive Dubai operating agreement during the six-year exclusivity period.
How far is Dubai Mall Vertiport from DXB airport?
Dubai Mall Vertiport is approximately 15 km by road from Dubai International Airport (DXB). A direct DXB ↔ Dubai Mall eVTOL route is part of the announced Dubai air taxi network.
Is Dubai Mall Vertiport open to the public yet?
No. Dubai Mall Vertiport was announced on 17 November 2025 and is in the planning phase. It is not yet operational. Commercial eVTOL service from Dubai Mall Vertiport is planned for 2026.
How much will an eVTOL flight from Dubai Mall cost?
Pricing for eVTOL flights from Dubai Mall Vertiport has not been published by Joby Aviation or the RTA. Booking will be available through Joby's dedicated app once service launches.
